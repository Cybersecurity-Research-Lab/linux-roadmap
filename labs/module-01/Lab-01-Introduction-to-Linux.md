# 💻 Lab 01 – Exploring the Linux Ecosystem

> Discover where Linux is used, explore the Linux ecosystem, and understand why it is one of the most important operating systems in modern computing.

---

## 📌 Lab Information

⏱️ **Estimated Time:** 20–30 minutes

🎯 **Difficulty:** Beginner

🟦 **Lab Type:** Theory & Exploration

📖 **Related Lesson:** [Lesson 01 – Introduction to Linux](../../docs/Module-01-Foundations/01-Introduction-to-Linux.md)

---

# 🎯 Objectives

By completing this lab, you will be able to:

* Identify where Linux is used.
* Recognize popular Linux distributions.
* Understand Linux's importance in cybersecurity.
* Explain why Linux dominates cloud computing.

# ☁️ Activity 4 – Why Does the Cloud Love Linux?

Think about the question before opening the answer.

> **Why do cloud providers such as AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud rely heavily on Linux?**

<details>

<summary><strong>✅ Show Explanation</strong></summary>

Linux is widely used in cloud computing because it is:

* Open source
* Stable
* Highly secure
* Efficient
* Easy to automate
* Flexible
* Supported by a large community

These qualities make Linux an excellent choice for running servers and cloud infrastructure.

</details>
